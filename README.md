# Spotify Playlist Analyzer

Analyze your Spotify playlists and discover deep insights about your music taste.

![Spotify Playlist Analyzer Dashboard](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/de0f461e-c84d-4ba6-9c32-a3b1619e9f30)

## Features

- **Top Artists** - See your most played artists across your playlists
- **Popular Songs** - Discover your most popular and frequently played tracks
- **Music Analysis** - Get detailed insights into your listening habits and preferences

## Tech Stack

- Python/Flask
- Spotify Web API
- HTML/CSS/JavaScript
